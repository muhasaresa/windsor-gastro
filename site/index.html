Consultant Gastroenterologist

Private gastroenterology care with consultant-led continuity.

I was appointed as consultant gastroenterologist at Heatherwood and Wexham Park Hospital in 2002 after specialist training in gastroenterology and general internal medicine across the North and South Thames London deanery.

My training included the Royal Free Hospital liver unit, Guy's and St Thomas' Hospital, and the Royal Sussex University Hospital in Brighton. I also completed two years of research at Guy's Hospital studying bile acids, intestinal bacteria, and gallstones.
