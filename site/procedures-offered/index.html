Procedures Offered

Advanced diagnostic and therapeutic endoscopy.

Dr Richard Sarsam completed specialist training in gastroenterology and general internal medicine across the North and South Thames London deanery.

Care includes a wide range of endoscopic diagnostics and interventions, tailored to symptom history, risk profile, and prior investigations.

Procedural focus

  • Gastroscopy Upper GI evaluation for reflux, pain, indigestion, and anaemia pathways.
  • Colonoscopy Diagnostic assessment, surveillance, and bowel cancer screening support.
  • Capsule endoscopy Investigation of small bowel pathology where clinically appropriate.
  • Therapeutic endoscopy Consultant-led treatment within a broader GI management plan.

Conditions treated

Common reasons for referral include rectal bleeding, altered bowel habit, abdominal pain, abnormal liver function tests, anaemia, acid reflux, jaundice, and indigestion (dyspepsia).

  • Rectal bleeding
  • Change in bowel habit
  • Abdominal pain
  • Abnormal liver tests
  • Anaemia
  • Acid reflux
  • Jaundice
  • Dyspepsia
